Head Tracking

System

Rest Mode

Put Pepper into low-power rest mode.

This will relax all motors and put Pepper in a resting position. Pepper will remain powered on but motors will be relaxed to conserve energy.

Restart Services

Restart all control services to apply updates or fix issues.

This will restart the control application and reconnect all services. Pepper will be unavailable for about 10-15 seconds.

Reboot Robot

Completely restart Pepper's operating system.

This will reboot the entire robot. All services will stop and Pepper will be unavailable for about 60-90 seconds.
